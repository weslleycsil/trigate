 (RESPOSTA-  indicando objeto)

1º- What  = o que  

English

Water

Coffee

Money

Bread

( 2º pergunta = indica o lugar)

2º- Where = onde

In the classroom

At home

In school

In the bank

In the kitchen

3º-When = quando (Tempo)

Every day

In the morning

In the break

Every month

At snack

 4º-How= como (Maneira) 

Very hard

Very cold

Very sweet

In cash

With jelly

5º-Why= Por quê? (Causa)

Because I like to.

Because I’m thirsty.

Because it’s good.

Because it’s necessary.

Because I’m hungry.

1 – ( O que você estuda? )

-What do you study?

-I study English.

-Where do you study English?

-I study  English in the classroom.

-When do you study English in the classroom? 

-I study  English in the classroom every day.

-How do you study English in the classroom every day? 

–I study English in the classroom every day very hard.

–Why do you study English in the classroom every day very hard?   

-I study English in the classroom every day very hard because I  like to. 

2 – ( O que você bebe? )

-What do you drink?

–I drink water.

-Where do you drink water?

-I drink water at home.

-When do you drink water at home?

-I drink water at home in the morning.

-How do you drink water at home in the morning?

-I drink water at home in the morning very cold.

-Why do you drink water at home in the morning very cold? 

-I drink water at home in the morning very cold because I’m thirsty.   

3 – ( O que você quer? )

-What do you want?

–I want coffee.

-Where do you want coffee?

–I want coffee in school.

-When do you want coffee in school?

-I want coffee in school in the break.

-How do you want coffee in school in the break? 

-I want coffee in school in the break very sweet.

-Why do you want coffee in school in the break very sweet?

-I want coffee in school in the break very sweet because it’s good. 

4 – ( O que você precisa? )

-What do you need?

-I need money.

-Where do you need money?

-I need money in the bank.

-When do you need money in the bank?

-I need money in the bank every month.

-How do you need money in the bank every month?

-I need money in the bank every month in cash.

-Why do you  need money in the

  bank every month in cash?

–I need money in the bank every

 month in cash because it’s necessary.
